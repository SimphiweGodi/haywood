DEPOSIT POLICY

Deposits are made to secure the date of an appointment as well as ensure an artist's time is not wasted if a cancelation or no-show is to occur. Deposits go toward the total cost of a tattoo. Just like a down payment. Any forms of pre-pay are considered a deposit and the same policy will apply.
• Deposits are 100% non-refundable.
• Deposits are always transferable with a 72-hour or more notice of a reschedule request, if a reschedule is requested the day before or the day of a tattoo appointment, the deposit is forfeited and a new one will be required to hold a new appointment.
Forfeiture of deposits occurs with cancelations, no-shows, and last-minute rescheduling.
By making an appointment and leaving a deposit with a tattoo artist at Haywood Tattoo Studio, you agree to the Haywood Tattoo Studio deposit policy.
